## 1. Executive Summary

Phase A of the WF Grid Search remediation is **stabilized and closed**. All mandatory fixes (Waves 1–3 and FIX-4.1) are implemented, tests are green, and the pipeline is accepted for research use with the caveats documented in the DISCLAIMER sheet.

## 3. Deferred Statistical Hardening Backlog

These items are collected here for Phase B planning. None of them are required to close Phase A. They represent additional statistical rigor beyond the current scope.

---

### FIX-4.2 — Minimum OOS Time Span Validation

**Problem**: No validation of total OOS duration. A grid run on 3 months of data produces structurally identical output to one on 3 years, with no warning.

**Intended solution**:
- After WF slicing, compute total OOS span in days/months.
- Config: `validation.min_total_oos_months` (default: 6 → warning; 12 → recommended).
- Log warning + record in diagnostics. Do not block pipeline.
- Add `total_oos_span_days` to `WF_Config` sheet.

---

### FIX-4.3 — Bootstrap Confidence Intervals for Top Candidates

**Problem**: Aggregate metrics (e.g. `sum_pnl_pct_Median`) have no confidence intervals. Point estimates on 3–5 WF steps are presented without uncertainty quantification.

**Intended solution**:
- `compute_bootstrap_ci(step_oos_long, grid_point_id, metric, n_bootstrap=1000, ci=0.95)`.
- Compute for top-N candidates (config: `ranking.compute_ci_for_top_n`, default: 10).
- Add `sum_pnl_pct_Median_CI_lower` and `sum_pnl_pct_Median_CI_upper` to summary.

---

### FIX-4.4 — Random Baseline Comparison

**Problem**: No comparison against a random strategy. High score relative to other grid points does not mean better than random.

**Intended solution**:
- Permutation test: `n_random=100`, `seed=42`.
- Compute `baseline_pnl_median`, `baseline_pnl_p05`, `baseline_pnl_p95`.
- Add `exceeds_random_baseline` boolean column.
- Publish baseline stats to `WF_Config` sheet or a dedicated sheet.

---

### FIX-4.5 — Temporal / Regime Consistency Analysis

**Problem**: No split between early and late OOS segments. A strategy that worked in the first half and failed in the second passes all current gates without any signal.

**Intended solution**:
- Compute `first_half_pnl_median` and `second_half_pnl_median` from `step_oos_long`.
- Derive `temporal_consistency = |first - second| / max(|first|, |second|, 1e-9)`.
- Add both to summary Block B.

---

### FIX-4.6 — Percentile-Based Scoring

**Problem**: Min-max normalization on small candidate pools (n < 5) produces false precision. Two near-identical candidates can receive scores of 0.0 and 1.0.

**Intended solution**:
- Config: `scoring.normalization` = `"minmax"` | `"percentile"` (default: `"minmax"`).
- Auto-switch to percentile rank when `n_passed < 5` with `normalization="minmax"`, log warning.
- Percentile rank: `norm(x) = percentile_rank(x) / (n_passed - 1)`.
